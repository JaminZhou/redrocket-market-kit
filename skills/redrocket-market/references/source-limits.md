# Red Rocket Source Limits

- Public endpoints may change without notice.
- Data can lag official exchanges, fund companies, or sales channels.
- Valuation percentiles and classifications are Red Rocket's own methodology unless otherwise documented by the site.
- Fund purchase limits, redemption status, fee rules, and settlement timing must be checked against fund companies or the actual sales channel before decision use.
- Use Red Rocket quote snapshots only for context. For intraday monitoring, use a dedicated real-time market source.
- Heat, news, wind-vane, and comparison outputs reflect Red Rocket page methodology and should be treated as discovery context, not as independent trading signals.
